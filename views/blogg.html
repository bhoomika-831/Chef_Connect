TRENDING RECIPE

One popular Asian takeout is General Tso's chicken. Deep-fried pieces of chicken coated in a crunchy coating, then tossed in sweet sauce, served over rice. The sweet sauce is actually really simple to make if you have a couple of Asian cooking ingredients in your kitchen: hoisin sauce and rice vinegar. You could use apple cider vinegar. To make this a quicker option, prepare the sauce in a skillet ahead of time and store it in a jar until you are ready to use it.
