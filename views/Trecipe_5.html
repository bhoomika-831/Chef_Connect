Strawberry Blueberry Smoothie

Ingredients needed: 3 cups Strawberries, fresh or frozen OR any mix of red berries 1 cup Blueberries, fresh or frozen 1-2 cups Water, adjust for your preferred thickness Splash Lemon juice, to taste Ice to taste 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt, fat level is your choice 1 cup / 107 g Cauliflower OR Spinach, if you dont mind the yucky color that results 1 Tbs ground Flaxseed (about 7 grams) 1 Tbs / 4 g Pumpkin seeds 1/4 to 1 cup / 20 g to 100 g Oats, adjust the calories as necessary


How to Make Strawberry Blueberry Smoothie

Blend all the ingredients mentioned above until smooth and enjoy!
